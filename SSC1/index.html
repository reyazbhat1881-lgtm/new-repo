About SSC Typing Test

SSC Typing Test is a free online platform designed to help candidates prepare for typing tests required in various SSC examinations including CGL, CHSL, and MTS.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality typing practice that follows official SSC standards, helping thousands of aspirants improve their typing speed and accuracy.

Key Features

  • 15-minute tests following SSC standards
  • Real-time speed and accuracy tracking
  • Multiple test passages for varied practice
  • English and Hindi language support
  • Free and unlimited practice
